Academic Notebook is a powerful, web-based note-taking application designed specifically for students, researchers, and academics. It combines traditional note-taking with specialized tools for mathematics, computer science, medicine, and more.

2. Feynman Technique

Learn by teaching - explain concepts in simple terms

Steps

  1. Choose Concept: What are you learning?
  2. Explain Simply: Write as if teaching a child
  3. Identify Gaps: What don't you understand?
  4. Review & Simplify: Refine your explanation

Features

  • Readability Analysis: Checks if explanation is simple enough
  • Jargon Detection: Highlights complex terms
  • Gap Tracking: Lists areas needing more study
  • Iteration Counter: Track improvement over time
